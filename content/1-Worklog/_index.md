**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Learning more about VPC , Route53, DNS and the other services in AWS](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [ Working with S3, CloudFront and AWS Storage Services](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [S3 Backup, Hybrid Storage Gateway, and FSx File System Management](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [AWS Architecture Design, Security Foundations & EC2/IAM Practical Operations](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [AWS Core Services & AI/ML Exploration](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Advanced AWS – Data Analytics, Security, Monitoring, Governance, and Pricing](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Practical AWS Labs – Security, Compute, Storage, Networking, and Database Deployment](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Hands-On AWS – Networking, Databases, Analytics, and Serverless Applications](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Hands-On AWS – Datalake, Analytics, QuickSight, Security, and AI/ML Services](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [AWS Containers, Serverless, and Advanced Cloud Services](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [AWS Networking, Backup, and DevTools Practice](1.12-week12/)
