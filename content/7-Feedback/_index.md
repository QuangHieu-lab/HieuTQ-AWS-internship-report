> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud Journey program. This will help the FCJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
Everyone here is very friendly and open. They are willing to share their AWS knowledge, and we also have fun moments together, creating many great memories while working.

**2. Love and Support from Mentors**  
The mentors are very friendly. They taught us many things about AWS, provided useful knowledge, and guided us on how to use AWS services. They also gave us helpful suggestions whenever we faced any problems.

**3. Improving Skills and Work Discipline**  
The tasks assigned are very basic and easy to follow. I was able to learn new knowledge about AWS services and improve my soft skills.

**4. Opportunities to Meet Skilled and Experienced People in the Company**  
During the OJT period, I had the opportunity to meet and get to know people with deep IT and AWS expertise. I also learned about their project experiences, the difficulties they faced, and the optimal solutions they used to become more professional in their work.

### Additional Questions

- What are you **most satisfied with** during your internship?  
- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
- If you introduce this place to your friends, would you **recommend them to intern here**? Why?

### Answers

- What I am most satisfied with is having the chance to study in a professional working environment, make new friends, and meet people who are more skilled than me.  
- The company should improve up-to-date lesson and provide more lessons about to AWS services.  
- I would encourage others to join this program because it is a great place to develop programming skills and also improve other abilities such as soft skills and work discipline.
